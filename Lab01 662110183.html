Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tall about it,and these are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter
    •

    Directions

    1. Mix eggs,sugar,and butter in a large bowl.
    2. Sread into a non-stick dish
    3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
    4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
    5. Eat and enjpy!
